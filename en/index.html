about us

Young company - bricklayers with many years of experience

The company was founded in February 2023. From the beginning, the company has employed bricklayers with 3-7 years of work experience who have successfully completed dozens of projects in Lublin and the surrounding area.

Our masters have experience in laying walls made of ceramic bricks, silicate blocks, foam blocks, as well as foundation blocks.
